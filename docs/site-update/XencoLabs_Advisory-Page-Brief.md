**H2:** The wall every developer hits

### SECTION 3 — WHAT WE DELIVER (navy)

**H2:** Four engagements. One standard: built to be decided on.

Four cards. **No prices on the public page** — engagement terms belong in the briefing (preserves founding-partner flexibility, and peers at Colossus/JLL altitude don't publish pricing either). Timelines and outcomes do the qualifying:

1. **Market Screening** — Per state or region. Ranked A/B/C sub-markets, zoning and moratorium landscape, hyperscaler activity, top counties to pursue. *Delivered in 2 weeks.*
2. **Site Pre-Qualification** — Per site. Carrier validation, true-diversity confirmation, dual-path routing design, construction and interconnection cost modeling, go/no-go recommendation. *Delivered in 30 days.*
3. **Full Feasibility** — Per site. On-site work, carrier negotiations, right-of-way validation, execution roadmap. *60–90 days, scoped per site.*
4. **Site Origination** — We source and pre-screen off-market opportunities matched to your acquisition criteria. ***Success-fee only: nothing due unless you acquire.*** (Yes, publish this one — the zero-risk structure is itself the hook.)

**Scope-boundary line (trust signal):** Pre-construction engineering — geotechnical, structural, MEP — is intentionally outside our practice. At that stage we scope and coordinate Tetra Tech-class firms, so you engage the right specialist at the right time.
